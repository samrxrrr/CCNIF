
METHODS

Framework
---------
Cancer Causal Network Inference Framework (CCNIF) v1.0

Cohort
------
TCGA-LUAD

Differential Expression
-----------------------
Minimum CPM: 1
Minimum sample fraction: 0.2
Transformation: log2(CPM+1)

DESeq2
------
Prefilter minimum count: 10
Prefilter sample fraction: 0.2
Adjusted P-value cutoff: 0.05
Log2 Fold Change cutoff: 1.0
Shrinkage method: apeglm

Functional Enrichment
---------------------
Organism: Homo sapiens
Adjusted P-value cutoff: 0.05

Gene Set Enrichment Analysis
----------------------------
Collection: H
Minimum pathway size: 15
Maximum pathway size: 500
Permutations: 10000
Ranking metric: signed_log10_padj

Production Pipeline
-------------------
The production workflow consisted of transcriptomic evidence generation,
clinical evidence integration,
functional enrichment,
network analysis,
survival analysis,
statistical modeling,
reliability estimation,
normalization,
quality assessment,
confidence scoring,
driver report generation,
and master index construction.
